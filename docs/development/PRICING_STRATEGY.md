#### Why This Price Point?

**1. Value-Based Pricing**
- **Time savings**: Saves hours of manual measurement work

- **Below premium tools** ($15-20/month) - More accessible

**3. Psychological Pricing**
- **$9.99** feels like single digits (vs. $10)
- **Under $10** is impulse purchase territory for professionals
- **$99/year** = "less than $100" psychological barrier

**4. Conversion Optimization**
- **Low enough** to convert free users easily
- **High enough** to filter serious users
- **Sustainable** for business growth

## ğŸ“ˆ Discount Strategy

### Why Offer Discounts?

**1. Reduce Churn**
- Longer commitments = lower churn
- Annual plans have ~50% less churn than monthly

**2. Improve Cash Flow**
- Upfront payment improves cash flow
- Reduces payment processing fees

**3. Increase LTV (Lifetime Value)**
- Longer commitments = higher total revenue
- Better unit economics

**4. Psychological Benefits**
- Users feel they're getting a deal
- "Save 33%" is compelling messaging

### Discount Tiers

| Plan | Price | Monthly Equivalent | Savings | Discount % |
|------|-------|-------------------|---------|------------|
| **Monthly** | $9.99/month | $9.99 | - | 0% |
| **6-Month** | $49.99/6mo | $8.33 | $10.00 | 17% |
| **Annual** | $79.99/year | $6.67 | $40.00 | 33% |

**Discount Rationale:**
- **6-month (17%)**: Moderate discount, encourages commitment
- **Annual (33%)**: Significant savings, maximum commitment
- **Sweet spot**: 30-35% is industry standard for annual discounts
